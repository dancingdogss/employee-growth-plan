// The key difference
Capital The 300 coins per unit is what I trust you to sell. It is not yours to keep. It returns to me once sold. You never earn the 300.
Earnings The 100 coins per unit sold is your actual growth — split between deposit and salary. Deposit helps you unlock more stock capacity. Salary is what you can actually use.
100 earned = deposit share + salary share
📈 Every 300 deposit = roughly +1 extra unit I can lend you. 1,500 deposit = +5 units capacity. 3,000 deposit = +10 units. That is why we build deposit first. The first goal is simple: reach 20 units sold.
